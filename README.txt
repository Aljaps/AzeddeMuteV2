# 🎙️ AzeddeMute

**AzeddeMute** is a lightweight Windows app for anyone who's always on meetings, gaming, or streaming — and needs a **quick way to mute/unmute their microphone** without clicking around.

Just press one key on your keyboard, and your mic is muted. That simple.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔇 **Toggle Mute** — Click the button or press your shortcut to instantly mute/unmute your mic
- ⌨️ **Custom Shortcut** — Set any keyboard key as your global hotkey
- 🖱️ **Push-to-Talk Mode** — Hold the key = mic on, release the key = mic muted automatically
- 🟢 **System Tray Icon** — Runs in the background with a color-coded tray icon (red = muted, green = active)
- 💻 **No Installation Required** — Just run the `.exe` file, no setup needed

---

## 🚀 How to Use

### 1. Launch the App
- Double-click `AzeddeMute.exe`
- The app will appear in the center of your screen

### 2. Set Your Own Shortcut
- Click the **"Setup Shortcut"** button at the bottom
- Press the keyboard key you want to use
- Click OK — your shortcut is now active

### 3. Mute / Unmute Your Mic
- **Click the mic area** in the middle of the app, **OR**
- Press your **shortcut key** (default: `F6`)

### 4. Switch Mode
- Click the **"MODE"** card to toggle between:
  - **Microphone** — press shortcut = toggle mute/unmute
  - **Push-to-Talk** — hold shortcut = mic on, release = mic off

### 5. Minimize to Tray
- Click the yellow button (top right corner) to minimize to the system tray
- The app keeps running in the background
- The tray icon changes color based on your mic status

### 6. Exit the App
- Click the red button (top right corner), **OR**
- Right-click the tray icon → **Exit**

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- The shortcut works **globally** — it works even when you're focused on another app (game, browser, etc.)
- When the app is closed, your mic is automatically **unmuted**
- Only supports **Windows** (not available for Mac/Linux)
